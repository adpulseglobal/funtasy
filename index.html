FAQ

Got questions?

Are the paints safe for kids?

Yes! We use only professional, hypoallergenic, FDA-compliant face paints that are water-based and easy to remove.

How many faces per hour?

Typically 10–15 simple designs or 6–8 full-face designs per hour, depending on complexity.

How far do you travel?

We serve the greater Ottawa area including Kanata, Nepean, Orleans, Barrhaven, and Gatineau. Travel fees may apply beyond 30km.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 25% deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remainder is due on the day of the event.

What if it rains (outdoor event)?

We're flexible! We can adapt to covered spaces or reschedule. We'll work with you to find a solution.

How far in advance should I book?

Summer and holidays fill up fast — 3–4 weeks ahead is ideal, though we do our best for last-minute requests.
